**Hobby renderer**
Some renderings of my hobby renderer. It is a path tracer that started as a project for college and then I improved for fun. It was developed from the ground up, starting from the math library, and it features kd-tree acceleration structures, multithread execution, importance sampling, approximated subsurface scattering and SDFs rendering. ![Moka](images/moka.png) ![My desk](images/desk.png) ![Head](images/head.png)
